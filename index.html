What is the KRA returns deadline?Tarehe ya mwisho ya KRA ni lini? ▼
KRA annual income tax returns are due by June 30 each year. Filing late attracts a penalty of Ksh 2,000 or 5% of tax due, whichever is higher.
Marejesho ya kodi ya KRA huisha Juni 30 kila mwaka. Kuchelewa huleta faini ya Ksh 2,000.
